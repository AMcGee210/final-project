The Local Shops

What shops are best for what rider

Cycles Ect

Cycles Ect is going to be your go to shop if you are willing to break the bank. Their line up of bikes is nothing but the top end carrying brands like Santa Cruz, Yeti, Juliana, and Ibis. They do carry brands that make more affordable options such as Giant and Trek, but seem to dote on the more expensive models. This is also a great shop to go to if you already own a high end bike and want quality replacement parts and service.

Link to Cycles Ect Website

The Bike Barn

The bike barn is a small shop that has a good beginner bike selection and even used bikes available. They find a niche for themselves being a Specialized dealer. In recent years Specialized has grown to be a top competitor in all ranges of bikes. Having such a good name being a Specialized dealer is valuable especially when no other shop in the area carries Specialized.

Link to The Bike Barn's Website

Goodales Bike Shop

Goodale's Bike Shop has recently been bought out by Trek. Despite this their operations have stayed nearly the same considering they were already a mainly Trek vendor. They have a huge selection of bikes at Goodales ranging from the highest end bikes to beginner bikes. Because of their wide range of possible customers it's a great shop to go to for bike tune ups and service no matter the price range of your bike.

Link to Goodale's Website
