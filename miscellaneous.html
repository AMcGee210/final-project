Important things to know

Random Knowledge You Normally Learn the Hard Way

  • Gloves can be super helpful, odly enough in warm weather rather than cold
  • Don't be cheap with a helmet, mips technology is recomended to have
  • Make sure you have a tire pump, and if you have air shocks buy a shock pump
  • Riding in the rain can be miserable, and can also upset other riders due to damage in wet conditions
  • If possible covert to tubless tires
  • Install a dropper seat post as soon as you can
  • If using platform pedals over clipless only use metal peg petals, no plastic
  • Get a pair of shoes that will grip to pedals well, Vans are a cheap option for this
  • Wash and take care of your bike as often as possible
