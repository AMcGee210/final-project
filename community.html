How to find friends to ride with

The Best Ways to Find Riders at Your Skill Level

The most effective ways to find other riders within your skill level is to use an app called strava, meet people on the trails, or join a facebook group or any other media platform that sets up group rides. Strava is a great app to have. It tracks your time and distance throughout a ride, and also tracks average speed, top speed, and elevation gain. You can use this to your advantage when following other riders. It's easier to find riders at your relative skill level when they are getting roughly the same times on the same trails as you. Meeting others while on the trail can be much more rare to find someone at your skill level, but they can most likely connect you with people who are. Scheduled group rides are a great way to meet people too. Typically for group rides the average skill level of the group is revealed by the group admins when signups are released.
