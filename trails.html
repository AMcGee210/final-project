How to find trails around you

Finding New Trails At Home and While Riding

Searching at Home

Finding trails at home can be challenging. The best way to go about it is to search for trails around your location on a search engine like google or safari, then search the trail or trail system up on youtube. This method allows you to find trails online, sift out what trails look the best through listed pictures on the site, then go deeper by watching a video of someone else walking or riding the trail.

Searching on the Trails

Snapchat can be a huge help while on the trail. This is because of the feature snapmap. Snapmap allows you to see your current location while also marking all the trails surrounding you in orange. This can be super helpful if you don’t know the fastest route, or if you are in an unfamiliar trail system looking for a specific trail. I have found new trails and saved myself from getting lost using snap maps.
